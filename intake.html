Client Intake  ·  The Beginning of the Work

Before We Meet

A Map-Side Preparation

This is the first document of your work with me. It is where the map is drawn — from the terrain of your birth, the ancestors you already know, and the patterns you have already noticed. Take your time. There are no wrong answers. What you cannot yet name belongs here too.

Essentials

The ground we're standing on. Birth data anchors the astrological reading; contact information makes it possible to reach you.

Leave blank if you don't know it. We'll take it up in conversation.

Ancestors

Name the deceased ancestors you want to begin with. Even partial information helps — names, approximate dates, places, occupations, anything you can connect to a genealogical record. You do not need to fill every field. Three blocks are visible below; add more if you need them. If you have no ancestral information to offer yet, that is also true, and you can skip this section.

Living Frequency Patterns

Five questions about the patterns active in you right now. Write as much or as little as feels true. Be specific where you can — what repeats, where the body holds it, what cannot yet be named. Precision here is what allows the ancestral research to converge with what is alive in your present.

01Describe a pattern you notice repeating — a situation, dynamic, or threshold that keeps surfacing in your life. When did you first become aware of it, and what, if anything, has shifted since?

02Where does your body carry a sensation, symptom, or charge that you cannot fully account for through your own history? Describe the sensation and, if you can, when it arrived.

03What is one story, belief, or prohibition in your family that you have questioned? What changes for you when you try to step outside it, even briefly?

04What are you currently entering or completing? Describe the shape of this threshold — not what it feels like, but where you recognize it sitting in your life right now.

05What have you been trying to name — a pattern, knowing, inheritance, or signal — that no existing language quite meets?

Open Remarks

Anything the form did not hold. Questions, context, requests, what you want me to know before we meet.

When you submit, your intake reaches me directly. You will hear back within a few days — with scheduling, with any questions that surface, and with the next step.
